MEL ANDREWS

I am a philosopher of artificial intelligence investigating the human use and misuse of mathematical and computational tools in knowledge production and decision-making. I focus particularly on emerging applications of machine learning (ML) in scientific and social contexts, aiming to promote both epistemically and ethically sound practices.
